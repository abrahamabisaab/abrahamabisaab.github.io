What is Inventory Managment System

The Inventory Managment System is a system created to manage the inventory of people who buy or sell medical equipment /instruments.
The project was built for a doctor in Tripoli, Lebanon. The project took around 3 months to complete.

How does this Inventory Management System works?

The admin creates an account for the doctor. After creation, the doctor will login to his account to start setting up his inventory.

Setup and transactions are listed below:

  • Add/Delete/View Medical Instruments.
  • Add/Delete/View Clients.
  • Buy/Sell Medical Instruments.
  • Print invoices that summarize transactions between buyer and seller.
  • Print bills that show how much each transaction item costed.
  • Automated management of the quantity of all medical instruments.
  • Manage Prices of all medical instruments.
