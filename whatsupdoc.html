What is "What's Up Doc"?

What's Up Doc is a my senior project during my last semester at Lebanese International University. The purpose of this project was to fascilitate the process of booking appointments with your doctor. I started working on this project at the beginning of the Spring 2019 semester. Front End and Back End Development were performed my myself and the process took around 3 months.

How does "What's Up Doc" work?

In order to start booking appointments, you must create an account and state if you are a patient or a doctor. After signing up and filling all credentials, you will be able to sign in to your account.
In case you sign up as a patient, you will be able to perform multiple tasks including :

  • Add/Delete/View his own Appointments.
  • View doctors near his area.
  • Send documents to his doctor if requested.
  • Search for the doctor according to region or specialty.
  • Add a comment based on the doctorâ€™s performance.
  • View notifications sent by his doctor

In case you sign up as a doctor, you will be able to peform multiple tasks including :

  • Add/Edit/Delete/View Patients.
  • Add/Edit/Delete/View Appointments.
  • Receive documents from a patient
